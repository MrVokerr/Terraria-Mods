A work-in-progress passive skill tree mod inspired by Path of Exile.
Earn points from boss kills and spend them on powerful stat upgrades.
Includes melee, summoner, magic, ranged, and defensive paths with respec support.
You can Respec your passives by crafting a Orb of Respec at a workbench (x5 Amethyst + 1 Bottle)
Chat command /recalcpassives has also been added, allowing players to manually re-sync their progression if needed.
