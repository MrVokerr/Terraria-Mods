A work-in-progress passive skill tree mod inspired by Path of Exile and World of Warcraft.
Earn points from boss kills and spend them on powerful passive upgrades.
Includes melee, summoner, magic, ranged, and defensive paths with respec support.
You can Respec your passives by crafting a Orb of Respec at a workbench (x5 Amethyst + 1 Bottle)

Chat Commands:
/passives - Check your passive point status and see all defeated boss milestones
/recalcpassives - Manually re-sync your progression if needed
