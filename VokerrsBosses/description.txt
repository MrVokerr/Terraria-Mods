Vokerr's Bosses adds exciting new boss encounters to Terraria!

Currently features:
- Mr. Game and Watch: A retro 2D fighter who brings his Super Smash Bros. moveset to Terraria!

How to Summon:
Craft the "Save File" item using:
- 5 Ectoplasm (Post-Plantera Dungeon drop)
- 10 Glass
- 5 Soul of Light
- 5 Soul of Night
Crafted at a Mythril/Orichalcum Anvil

This boss is balanced for Post-Plantera/Duke Fishron tier progression.
